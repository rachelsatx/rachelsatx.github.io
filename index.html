When Life Gives You Lemons

This iPad game was the final group project in my Software Development course. My team of five students (with no experience in iOS development prior to the class) was instructed to create an educational social science iPad game with 5th graders as our target audience. We created this lemonade stand app as a fun way to introduce the concepts of economics and business management.

Matt Bomer 2048

This is a game I made over the course of a few evenings in 2014, slightly after the height of the 2048 craze. I had been learning about HTML and Javascript and wanted a chance to practice and learn while creating something I could play and share with others. I was still very new to Git, so it was also a learning experience trying to navigate GitHub.
